Track Fit Training is an athletic training development program for athletes aged 5 – 15 years.
Our Mission
Track Fit Training’s goal is to create a positive relationship with fitness and Track and Field. By offering athletic training programs that focus on running, jumping, throwing, speed and coordination in age-appropriate groups. Our track-based programing will improve fitness and aims to create a love for the sport.
